Agent Layer

Agents built on your methodology and domain knowledge. They operate across your accounts, picking up work proactively and executing it at your standard. They don't wait for instructions. They track, flag, start, and surface.

Governance Infrastructure

Your best work, systematized. Shadow captures how your senior people operate and encodes it into governance SOPs that run across every account. This keeps voice, flow, and approach consistent across engagements.

Always Progressing

Deliverables, onboarding sequences, and account programs move forward without being prompted.

Meets You Where You Are

Integrated with your stack: HubSpot, your CRM, and your existing tools. Notifications in Slack, email, or wherever your team works. Or move to Shadow's AI-native CRM built specifically for agency workflows.
